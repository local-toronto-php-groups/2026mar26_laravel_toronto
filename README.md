##
After one of the snowiest winters, it is time to have a spring meet-up.

Your fellow Toronto Artisans are curious about the new Laravel AI package.

So, we are going to do a hackathon-ish workshop. Or, a workshop-ish hackathon, to get our hands on it. 

The Laravel AI package was published as beta, on Feb 05th. It might be GA released by the 26th, or maybe not. It is a new package. 

These days, each developer has their own custom local development environment. Dev environments are more intense than ever. To the extent that if we go to the meet-up with incomplete local dev set-ups on our laptops, we could spend the entire meet-up on its completion. To keep the focus on AI apps, please complete your local set-up prior. 

You also need API access to your favourite LLM(s). Google offers free access to developers, for prototyping. No CC required. 

I am personally in the midst of developing an AI app for my podcasts. However, I am building my own custom functionality, in lieu of installing the new AI package. 

A very special thank you to those who are active on our Discord server -- it is a big help for me to hear from you outside of the meet-ups.

Thank you to Maple for hosting, and sponsoring the pizza.

Schedule:
- 6:00pm to 7:00pm: meet-and-greet
- 7:00pm to 9:00pm: presentation

-Bob
